The 10-Point Checklist

1.Has an llms.txt file
2.Has a sitemap.xml
3.Has Organization schema markup
4.Has LocalBusiness or Service schema markup
5.Has FAQPage schema markup
6.<title> tag describes what the business actually does (not "Home")
7.H1 names the service + location
8.Meta description is present and under 160 characters
9.robots.txt doesn't block AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, etc.)
10.About/Services page has real structured text content, not just images

A file that tells AI crawlers what your business does, in language they can use directly. It's one piece of a bigger picture — which is why our pack includes schema markup and content fixes too, not just this one file.
